About Us

Himanshu Patil is a resident of Vikhroli since early childhood.

After completing his education, he started his "Guru Diagnositic Center" at Vikhroli.

It was then the only such centre offering X-rays, Blood tests, Sonography and ECG under one roof.

PACKAGES

HEALTH CHECKUP PLANS 

Health Checkup (Regular)

Physical Examination, CBC, ESR, FBS, PPBS, LIPID PROFILE, SGPT, CREATININE, TSH, URINE ROUTINE, STOOL ROUTINE, X-RAY CHEST, and ECG.

Comprehensive Health Checkup

This plan offers all the tests of the health checkup plan plus liver function tests, Kidney function tests too.

Pre-employment Checkup

It has the following tests: Physical Examination CBC, ESR, Blood group, FBS, HIV, HBSAg, Lipid profile, Urine Routine, X-ray Chest, ECG.

Diabetic Profile

This offers FBS, PPBS, Glycosylated Hemoglobin, Urine Micro albumin.

Hypertension Profile

This plan offers Lipid profile, S.Creatinine, FBS, PPBS, and ECG

P.U. O (fever profile)

CBC, ESR, MP, WIDAL URINE, SGPT.

Pre-operative Profile

CBC, ESR, FBS, PPBS, VDRL, HIV, HBSAg, HCV, URINE ROUTINE, XRAY CHEST and ECG.

Antenatal Profile

Hb, Blood group, FBS, VDRL, HIV, HBSAg, HCV, URINE ROUTINE, TSH.
